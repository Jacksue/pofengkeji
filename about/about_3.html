Usama Fayyad
Usama Fayyad is Yahoo!'s chief data officer and executive vice president of Research & Strategic Data Solutions. Fayyad is responsible for Yahoo!'s overall data strategy, architecting Yahoo!'s data policies and systems, prioritizing data investments, and managing the Company's data analytics and data processing infrastructure. He also founded and currently oversees the Yahoo! Research organization globally.
Usama Fayyad, Yahoo 公司管理研究和战略数据解决方案方面首席数据运营官、行政副总裁，主要负责Yahoo的数据策略、原则及系统架构，引领数据开发并管理公司的数据分析及数据处理基础。同时，Fayyad创建并监管者Yahoo!全球研发机构。
Tarek Najm
Tarek Najm is a Technical Fellow working in the Search, Portal and Advertising Division at Microsoft. Najm’s current areas of focus include Web Semantics, Data Mining and Knowledge Engineering. A visionary architect, Najm founded and led worldwide development of several large-scale Web initiatives including: Microsoft adCenter Advertising Platform, PubCenter Platform for Publishers, adCenter Labs, Audience Intelligence for Data mining and Data Warehousing.
Tarek Najm是微软搜索、门户网站、广告部门的技术人员。Najm主攻领域主要有：语义网络、数据挖掘及知识工程。作为一个有远见的架构师，Najm创办并引领几个大型网站的全球发展：微软adCenter广告平台、PubCenter发行者平台， adCenter实验室，数据挖掘和数据仓库的用户智能网站。
Dr. Harry Shum
Former managing director of Microsoft Research Asia, Dr. Harry Shum, a Corporate Vice President at Microsoft now, has taken the new role of leading the Core Search Development of Microsoft.
Dr. Shum received a doctorate in robotics from the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, PA. Dr. Shum has published more than 100 papers in computer vision, computer graphics, pattern recognition, statistical learning, and robotics. He holds more than 50 U.S. patents.
Dr. Harry Shum曾任微软亚洲研发主管，现在是微软副总裁，主要负责微软核心搜索开发工作。
Dr. Shum 在宾夕法尼亚州匹兹堡的卡内基 - 梅隆大学计算机科学学院获得机器人学博士学位，发表了一百多篇有关计算机视觉，计算机图形学，模式识别，统计学习和机器人学等方面的论文，现拥有50多项专利。
Prof. Jian Pei
Jian Pei is currently an Associate Professor of Computing Science and an Associate Director at the School of Computing Science at Simon Fraser University, Canada. He joined the School of Computing Science at Simon Fraser University as a faculty member in 2004. In 2002 – 2004, he was an Assistant Professor of Computer Science and Engineering at the State University of New York (SUNY) at Buffalo, USA.
He received a Ph.D. degree in Computing Science from Simon Fraser University, Canada, in 2002, under Dr. Jiawei Han’s supervision. He also received a B. Eng. degree and a M. Eng. degree, both in Computer Science, from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China, in 1991 and 1993, respectively. He was a Ph.D. candidate in Peking University, China, in 1997-1999.
Jian Pei教授现任加拿大西蒙弗雷泽大学计算科学学院的计算机科学副教授，为该院的副主任。2004年，他加入西蒙弗雷泽大学，成为计算机科学学院的一名讲师。2002年至2004年，成为位于美国布法罗的纽约州立大学计算机科学与工程系的助理教授。
Jian Pei分别于1991年与1993年在上海交通大学取得了计算机科学学士&硕士学位。1997-1999年，成为北京大学的博士候选人。2002年，成为加拿大西蒙弗雷泽大学Jiawei Han博士旗下的一名博士生并取得了计算机科学专业博士学位。
